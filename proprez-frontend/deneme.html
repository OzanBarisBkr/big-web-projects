Region

Americas

From our office in Houston, Vitol continues to build a long-standing presence across the Americas. We are a major participant in several key markets including upstream and crude trading, as well as power and gas. In the US, we have a growing portfolio of renewables in solar and wind, optimising our assets by integrating them into our existing trading capacity.

Alongside a diverse product portfolio, a network of logistical infrastructure stretching through the Caribbean and into Latin America means we are well-placed to offer our customers optionality and ensure a secure supply of product.

Region

EMEA

A trusted partner across EMEA with long-standing relationships with both customers and suppliers. In Africa we are investing in infrastructure across midstream and downstream with a network of 2,400 petrol stations and over 1.6 m m3 of storage in 23 countries. In Europe, we are invested in 3 refineries and have a diverse portfolio of storage assets. We supply Europe’s power sector with power, LNG and gas, and have a broadening portfolio of renewable energy projects includes hydrogen and gas-for-transport projects.
